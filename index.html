Cooking & Food Science

Cooking has become one of my strongest interests because it combines creativity, problem-solving, and science. I enjoy learning how ingredients interact during preparation and how factors such as temperature, moisture, texture, and timing affect the final product.


Beyond following recipes, I like experimenting with different techniques and observing how changes in ingredients or preparation methods influence flavor, appearance, and consistency. These experiences have increased my interest in food science by helping me understand that food production involves chemistry, biology, safety, and quality control working together.


My long-term goal is to continue exploring the scientific side of food and gain experience in areas such as food development, food safety, nutrition, and food processing.
